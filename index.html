About DECAGON

DECAGON X SVIS

Decagon is more than simply an event; it's a celebration of creativity, innovation, and technology, organised by the bright minds of Sri Venkateshwar International School in Dwarka, New Delhi.

Some of the most talented young people come together at Decagon to compete, work together, and create in an exciting setting. Decagon, which consists of six intese events, aims to spark enthusiasm in a variety of IT fields.

At Decagon, we're committed to pushing boundaries and creating new standards. Every dreamer and performer has a place, regardless of whether they are programmers, gamers, artists, editors, or photographers.
